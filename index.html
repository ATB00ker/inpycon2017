Venue: IIM Lucknow (Noida Campus)

Dev Sprints: 2 - 3 November

Main Conference: 4 - 5 November

PyCon India is the premier volunteer-run python conference catering to python developers from India and abroad with the aim of cultivating the python community and its projects in and around the Indian Subcontinent.

Head over to the CFP section (coming soon) to submit a proposal if you would like to present talk / conduct a workshop or devsprint at the conference.

At PyCon India everyone buys a ticket, including the speakers and volunteers.

Previous Editions of Pycon India have been supported by companies including ZeOmega, Microsoft, Google, GoIbibo, Zopper, Red Hat etc. If you or your company would like to be a part of this elite list, feel free to drop a line on sponsorship_AT_in.pycon.org

For any other clarification, please feel free to touch base with the organizing team at contact_AT_in.pycon.org
